Hello Food: Food Ordering Website

Log in page


Backend database

Intro

Skills: HTML, CSS, PHP, JSON

This is a online food ordering process website, where users can purchase food delivery conveniently and companies can follow orders easily.
I was in charged of following works: Created interactive webpages, including menu selections and automatic price counting. Created adding dish page. Did users’ account page too, displaying the orders’ status and history. Improved database which connecting all user information. Developed whole site front-end work.

Functional Design
* User: Customers ordering food online.
- Login: identify his authentication to login
- Order: order food from restaurants
- Review: After food delivered, the user is allowed to score the ordered food
- My account: View the history of a user, and review the previous orders

* Seller: Restaurants providing delivering service.
- Login: identify his authentication to login
- View come-in order: New come-in orders are show on a Google map
- Confirm: By confirming order, let the users know their dishes are going to be cooked
- Deliver: Mark the order as delivered, the order won’t show on the sellerpage.(means the order has been completed)
- Logout: Go back to the welcome page
