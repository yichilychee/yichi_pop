SycnUp Application

Communication drops dramatically

Problem

Skills: Research

Face-to-face communications of people in the UM community with different backgrounds is reduced dramatically. High-quality friends are more and more difficult to make. What’s more, since the schedule openings are more flexible than what people plan ahead, everyone has many scattered free time slots. Most of these time slots have not been fully and meaningfully used. Last but not least, right now, events known by people in the UM community is limited and separate.

From all above, there is a need to have a comprehensive platform, by which all people in the UM community can find interesting and suitable offline events to attend. And then they can have more face-to-face communication, make more high-quality new friends and make full use of their schedule openings.

One part of the Sketches

Ideation & Sketch

Skills: Sketch

There are a couple of events updating apps in the market. I did an extensive competitive analysis and then self-brainstormed for different approaches. I created 40 alternative sketches, clustered these alternatives into a few high-level functional categories, and then decided to go in a different way from existing apps, add some new features and remove useless features.

One example of Personas

Personas & Scenarios

Skills: Persona, Scenario, Sketch

I developed 4 personas of sample users, built a scenario for each persona and designed a storyboard for the application. Personas helped me think about my solution in the physical context more specifically. Scenarios provided a fast and effective way to check the design concepts in use. I used personas and scenarios to guide my prototypes and to assess my designs throughout the whole project later on.

Examples of Paper Prototype

Wireframe & Paper Prototype

Skills: Wireframe, Prototype, POP

After finishing wireframe of the app, I created a paper prototype focusing on major tasks and interactions to collect feedbacks from peers. On this stage, I came up with different interaction possibilities, analyzed design synthesis, made design decisions. During the testing, I observed testee's behaviors and got many meaningful comments.

Different versions of Digital Prototypes

Digital Prototype

Skills: Axure

After revising and revising based on my continuous feedbacks gathering, in the digital prototype, I focused more on the major tasks, functions and interactions instead of the visual design but I still changed color pallete and edited appearance twice to make the app visually appealing.
